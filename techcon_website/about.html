Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of passionate developers and tech leaders. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants globally and shaping the direction of innovation.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is driven by a simple yet powerful mission: to foster innovation, share knowledge, and empower future leaders in technology. Our goal is to provide a collaborative space for learning, networking, and sparking ideas that change the world.
